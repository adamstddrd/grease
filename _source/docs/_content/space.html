.space

Where .grid is mostly concerned about horizontal layout, .space deals with vertical layout; albeit in a simpler way. .space doesn’t apply any default on its own, so you’ll always use it with a modifier class.

You can add @small or @medium to any of the space modifiers to have them apply only on the small or medium and large breakpoints; particularly useful when you need space between elements when they’re stacked but not when they’re side by side. You can use them in combination too, like class="space --top-m@small --top-flush@medium".
